This is about the game R6S. This game Rainbow Six Siege is multiplayer online game on pc and ps4. Different operators have different nationalities, weapons, and gadgets. Choose your operators for intense 5v5 team matches in 3 minutes. There are 11 maps, they all are exactly different but they all have good points. If you are bigginer you can play casual matches and when you will be over 20 rank, you can play rank matches. You have to get 4 rounds first to win the game. First 3 round you will play offence or defence and after 3rd round it will be changed. For example, in first 3 rounds you play defence and after 3rd round you play offence. When you get 3 rounds, enemies also get 3 rounds it is tied and it will be continued and it is changed to get 5 rounds first. It is still updating so you can start
